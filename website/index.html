About

Ishare is a platform that highlights the inclusion of deaf/dumb community by contracting the communication gap. It uses various methods like web chat application on video calling platforms, sign language translator and a basic course on sign language.

You can create your own custom avatar for the masthead, change the icon in the dividers, and add your email address to the contact form to make it fully functional!
